Pilau Recipe

ingredients

  • 2 table spoons oil
  • 300 grams beef cubes
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, grated
  • 1 table spoon chili powder
  • 1½ table spoon pilau masala
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1½ cup of water(to cook the beef)
  • 1½ cups rice rinsed and soaked
  • salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 bulb of garlic, cut the top off to expose the cloves of garlic
  • 1 table spoon coriander powder
  • 3 cups water(to cook the rice)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in large pot. Add the beef in the oil i batches making sure not to over crowd the pot. You want to sear the beef so it browns and not steams. Let cook for 3-4 minutes until browned on all sides
  2. Add the onions and stir for 1-2 minutes
  3. Add the carrots, seasoned with salt and pepper, chili powder, pilau masala and bay leaves and stir. Let cook for another 1-2 minutes or until the carrots softens
  4. Add hot water (to cook the beef), cover the pot with a lid and simmer for 45 minutes- 1 houruntil the meat is very tender.
  5. Later the rice onto the meat , season with some salt, pour in the water(to cook rice) and let simer uncover for 10-12 minutes.
  6. sprinkle coriander powder onto the almost cooked rice and push the bulb if garlic into the rice making sure that the exposed cloves are submerged in the rice
  7. Cover and reduce the temperatures and let sit for 12-15 minutes
  8. Remove the bulb of garlic and bay leaves from the rice, and serve the pilau while hot.
